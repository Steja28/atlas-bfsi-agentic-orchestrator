# Atlas Adoption Playbook

**Purpose:** Guide business teams and leadership through the adoption of the Atlas agentic orchestrator platform for BFSI review workflows.

---

## 1. Pilot Rollout Plan

### Phase 1: Q3 Pack Pilot (30 days)

**Scope:**  
- Pilot Q3 review pack workflow in one business unit (e.g., Retail Lending)  
- Define success criteria: cycle time reduction, rework reduction, user satisfaction  

**Success Metrics:**  
- Baseline: Manual Q3 pack takes 5-7 days analyst time  
- Target: Atlas-assisted pack completes in 2-3 days with 30-40% less rework  

### Phase 2: Credit Committee Extension (60 days)

**Scope:**  
- Extend to credit committee review packs  
- Incorporate compliance feedback and risk officer review  

**Success Metrics:**  
- Reduce credit pack preparation time by 25%  
- Achieve 100% audit trail completeness

## 2. RACI Including Agents

| Activity | Agent Responsible | Human Accountable | Human Consulted | Human Informed |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Draft deck slides | DeckAgent | Analyst | BU Head | PMO |
| Final deck content | Analyst | BU Head | Risk, Ops | Exec Team |
| Propose attendees | CRMAgent | Analyst | BU Head | - |
| Finalize attendee list | Analyst | BU Head | - | PMO |
| Schedule meeting | CalendarAgent | PMO | BU Head | All attendees |
| Post-meeting actions | NotesAgent | PMO | BU Head | Action owners |

---

## 3. Guardrails

**Recommend-only actions** (agents suggest, humans decide):  
- Proposed attendees for meetings  
- Risk statement wording in decks  
- Covenant breach interpretations  

**Auto-execute actions** (agents can act without approval):  
- Internal draft email notifications  
- Calendar holds for proposed times  
- Data pulls from approved systems  

**Never automate**:  
- External communications to customers or regulators  
- Credit decisions or limit changes  
- Policy exceptions  

---

## 4. Change Management

- Train analysts on "AI as teammate" workflows  
- Establish feedback loops to improve agent quality  
- Monitor adoption metrics and iterate based on user input
